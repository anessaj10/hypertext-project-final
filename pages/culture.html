🌿Guyana

baigan = eggplant.
choka = a Hindi word.
the technique of finishing something over heat.

a roasted eggplant dip,
originating from South Asia culinary traditions,
popularized across the Caribbean,
particularly in Guyana and Trinidad and Tobago.
one generation at a time.

Known as the only English-speaking country in South America
and a member of CARICOM (Caribbean Community).
This land of many waters is home to a rich tapestry of cultures,
including Indigenous peoples, Afro-Guyanese, Indo-Guyanese, and more.
