how he makes it

🔥 the recipe

whole eggplant. with some garlic stuck in them
straight to the flame (or wrapped in foil).
let the skin burn.

while it chars, he preps —
black pepper, wiri wiri pepper, a pinch of salt.
no measuring. he just knows.

when the skin is black and the inside soft,
he peels it back.
what's left is creamy, smoky, warm.

he stirs it all together.
and that's it!
eat it fresh. its the best that way.
