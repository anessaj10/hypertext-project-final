how she makes it

🫓 the recipe

she measures everything.
flour, water, oil, salt, baking powder.

she works the dough until it gives —
soft, smooth, never overworked.
let rest a little (this ensures the softness).
patience is part of the recipe too.

she rolls it round and flattens with a rolling pin.
the tawa is already hot.
she doesn't rush.

the dough swells and
she flips it once.
soft, warm, and fluffy.
